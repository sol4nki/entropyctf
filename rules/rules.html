General Rules

  • Teams may consist of 1–4 members
  • Flag format is always: flag{...}
  • Each challenge can only be solved once per team
  • Brute forcing challenge infrastructure is prohibited
  • Sharing flags or solutions is strictly forbidden

Prohibited Activities

  • Attacking other teams or their infrastructure
  • Denial of service attacks on challenge servers
  • Exploiting vulnerabilities in the competition platform itself
  • Using multiple accounts or team registration manipulation
  • Any form of cheating or collaboration between teams

Scoring

  • Points are awarded immediately upon correct flag submission
  • Challenges may decrease in value based on solve count
  • Ties are broken by time of last solve
  • Wrong flag submissions may result in temporary lockout

Conduct

  • Be respectful to organizers and other participants
  • Ask for hints in the designated channels if stuck
  • Report any technical issues or bugs immediately
  • Have fun and learn something new
